Gukundisha Abanyeshuri Amasomo y’Ingenzi

Abarezi berekanye ko gukundisha abanyeshuri amasomo y’ingenzi bifasha guteza imbere ireme ry’uburezi mu gihugu. Gukundisha abanyeshuri amasomo byongera ubushake bwo kwiga no gukurikirana neza amasomo atandukanye, bigatuma umwana yiga neza kandi agatera imbere mu bumenyi.

Ikigo cy’uburezi cyagaragaje ko uburyo bwo guha abanyeshuri inyigisho zishimishije, harimo ibikorwa by’imikino, imfashanyigisho zishimishije, n’ibikorwa by’ubugeni, bifasha mu gutuma abanyeshuri bagira ubushake bwo kwiga no kumenya byinshi mu masomo y’ingenzi.

Abarezi bakomeje kwibutsa ko gukundisha amasomo atandukanye bifasha kandi mu kubaka ubumenyi bwimbitse, ubunyangamugayo, no gufasha abana gukura bafite ubushake bwo gukurikirana ubumenyi n’imyigire yabo neza.

Urwego rw’abashinzwe uburezi rugerageza gukorana n’abarezi mu gukoresha uburyo bugezweho bwo kwigisha, harimo ikoranabuhanga, imfashanyigisho z’amashusho, n’imyitozo y’imyidagaduro yongera ubumenyi. Ibi byose bigamije guha abanyeshuri uburyo bwo kwiga bufite ireme kandi bushimishije.
