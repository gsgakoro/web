GS GAKORO RIASEC TEST

1. I like to work on cars
(Nkundira gukora ku mamodoka)

2. I like to do puzzles
(Nkundira gukemura udushushanyo duhuje)

3. I am good at working independently
(Mbasha gukora ntari kumwe n’abandi)

4. I like to work in teams
(Nkundira gukorera mu itsinda)

5. I am an ambitious person, I set goals for myself
(Ndi umuntu wifuza kugera kure, nshyiraho intego)

6. I like to organize things (files, desks/offices)
(Nkundira gutunganya ibintu (amadosiye, ibiro))

7. I like to build things
(Nkundira kubaka ibintu)

8. I like to read about art and music
(Nkundira gusoma ibijyanye n’ubugeni n’umuziki)

9. I like to have clear instructions to follow
(Nkundira kubona amabwiriza asobanutse yo gukurikiza)

10. I like to try to influence or persuade people
(Nkundira kugerageza kugiraho abandi ingaruka)

11. I like to do experiments
(Nkundira gukora igerageza)

12. I like to teach or train people
(Nkundira kwigisha cyangwa guhugura abantu)

13. I like trying to help people solve their problems
(Nkundira gufasha abantu gukemura ibibazo byabo)

14. I like to take care of animals
(Nkundira kwita ku nyamaswa)

15. I wouldn’t mind working 8 hours per day in an office
(Nta kibazo kuba nakora amasaha 8 ku munsi mu biro)

16. I like selling things
(Nkundira kugurisha ibintu)

17. I enjoy creative writing
(Nkundira kwandika bijyanye n'ubuhanzi)

18. I enjoy science
(Nkundira siyansi)

19. I am quick to take on new responsibilities
(Nihutira kwakira inshingano nshya)

20. I am good at keeping records of my work
(Mbasha kubika inyandiko z’ibyo nakoze)

21. I like to analyze things (problems/situations)
(Nkundira gusesengura ibintu (ibibazo/ibikorwa))

22. I like to play instruments or sing
(Nkundira gucuranga cyangwa kuririmba)

23. I enjoy learning about other cultures
(Nkundira kwiga ku mico y’ahandi)

24. I would like to start my own business
(Nifuza gutangiza ubucuruzi bwanjye)

25. I like to cook
(Nkundira guteka)

26. I like to act in plays
(Nkundira gukina amakinamico)

27. I am a practical person
(Ndi umuntu uharanira gushyira mu bikorwa)

28. I like working with numbers or charts
(Nkundira gukorana n’imibare cyangwa imbonerahamwe)

29. I like to get into discussions about issues
(Nkundira kujya mu biganiro ku bibazo bihari)

30. I am good at keeping things neat and organized
(Mbasha gutunganya ibintu neza no kubishyira ku murongo)
