Welcome to EduGuide

The EduGuide System is a digital platform designed to help learners at GS GAKORO discover and follow their ideal learning pathways. It provides tools for career guidance, academic progress tracking, and personalized recommendations, enabling students to understand their strengths and interests. Teachers and administrators can also monitor student performance and engagement, ensuring timely support and guidance. Ultimately, EduGuide empowers learners to make informed decisions about their education and future careers while fostering a culture of continuous growth and achievement.
